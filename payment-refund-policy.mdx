**Payment Terms**\
To ensure a smooth workflow and commitment from both sides, the following payment structure applies:\
\- Projects under $10 USD\
Full payment is required **upfront** before any work begins.\
\- Projects $10 USD and above\
A **50% upfront payment** is required to start the project. The remaining **50% must be paid upon completion** before final delivery.
