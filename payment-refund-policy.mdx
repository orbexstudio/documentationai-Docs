## 💳 Payment Terms

To ensure commitment from both sides, the following payment structure applies:

- **Projects under $10 USD**\
  Full payment is required **upfront** before any work begins.

- **Projects $10 USD and above**

  - **50% upfront payment** is required to start the project

  - Remaining **50% must be paid upon completion** before final delivery

---

## 🚫 Refund Policy

- Once a project has been **confirmed and work has started**, **no refunds will be issued**.

- This policy ensures time, effort, and resources are respected on both sides.

---

## ⚠️ Additional Notes

- Work will **not begin** until the required upfront payment is received.

- Final files, access, or deliverables will only be provided after **full payment is completed**.
